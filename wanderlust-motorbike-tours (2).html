The Grand Northeast Loop

Popular Routes & Destinations

Explore North-East India on Two Wheels

🏍️ Tawang Bike Tour

The ultimate Tawang bike tour from Guwahati traverses Bhalukpong, Bomdila, Dirang and the breathtaking Sela Pass (4,170m) before reaching Tawang Monastery. Our Tawang package tours include all Inner Line Permits, Royal Enfield bikes and expert local guides.

🗺️ Anini Adventure

The Anini Adventure takes you deep into the Dibang Valley — one of India's last true wilderness frontiers. Anini, the remotest district headquarters in the country, is accessible only by this legendary forest road. A bucket-list Arunachal tourism experience.

🎵 Ziro Music Festival Ride

Ride to the Ziro Music Festival in Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh — India's most unique outdoor music event set among Apatani rice fields and pine-covered hills. Our Ziro Music Festival bike tour combines culture, music and high-altitude riding.

🥁 Hornbill Festival Ride

The Hornbill Festival ride is our most culturally immersive tour — a 10-day motorcycle journey to Kohima, Nagaland during the famous Hornbill Festival in December. Witness 16 Naga tribes perform in traditional dress while camping in the Naga hills.

🛣️ Guwahati to Tawang

The Guwahati to Tawang route is 540km of pure Himalayan riding magic — crossing three major passes, three river valleys and dozens of army checkpoints into one of the most restricted yet beautiful regions of India. We manage all permits for this Arunachal tourism route.

🌿 Arunachal Tourism

Arunachal Pradesh tourism on a motorbike is the most authentic way to experience the Land of the Rising Sun. From Tawang's Buddhist monasteries to the tribal heartlands of Arunachal, our guided motorcycle tours unlock destinations that most tourists never reach.

Common Questions

FAQs — Tawang Bike Tour & More

How do I book a Tawang bike tour from Guwahati? +

WhatsApp us at +91 91011 59268 or fill our booking form above. We handle all Inner Line Permits, Royal Enfield bikes, accommodation on the Guwahati to Tawang route and full mechanical support.

What is the Anini Adventure tour? +

The Anini Adventure is a guided motorbike expedition through Dibang Valley to Anini — the remotest district headquarters in India. It requires restricted area permits which we arrange. This is one of the most extreme and rewarding Arunachal tourism experiences available.

When is the Hornbill Festival Ride? +

The Hornbill Festival in Kohima, Nagaland takes place every year in the first week of December. Our Hornbill Festival ride is a 10-day motorcycle tour timed around this event. Book early — seats are limited and this is our most popular festive tour.

Can I join the Ziro Music Festival on a bike tour? +

Yes! The Ziro Music Festival is held every September in Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh. Our Ziro Music Festival ride combines stunning mountain riding through Arunachal with 4 days of indie music, camping and Apatani tribal culture. One of a kind.

Do I need riding experience for Tawang package tours? +

Our Tawang package tours are graded by difficulty. The Guwahati to Tawang route is rated intermediate-advanced due to high altitude passes. We offer beginner-friendly tours in Meghalaya and Assam. All riders receive pre-tour briefings and ride with our experienced guides.
